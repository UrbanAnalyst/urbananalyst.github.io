Urban Transport Analyst

GitHub

A tool to analyse
the social equitability of
urban transport systems

Urban transport will change rapidly across the world in the coming years, yet access to good walking and cycling paths, and to public transport, are not equally distributed throughout most cities. Urban Transport Analyst is a tool to assess the social equity of transport infrastructure. The effects of any proposed changes to transport infrastructure can be assessed in terms of their social equitability, both as aggregated across entire cities, and in fine-scaled local detail.
