Urban Transport Analyst

GitHub

A tool to analyse the social equitability of urban transport systems

Urban transport will change rapidly across the world in the coming years, especially by encouraging and enabling active movement (such as walking and cycling), and the use of public transport. Access to good walking and cycling paths, and to public transport, are however not equally distributed throughout most cities. Urban Transport Analyst is a tool to assess the social equity of transport infrastructure. This tool will enable the effects of any proposed changes to transport infrastructure to be assessed in terms of their social equitability, both as aggregated across entire cities, and in fine-scaled local detail.

Development is scheduled to begin in September 2022.
