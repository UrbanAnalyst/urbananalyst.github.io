Urban Transport Analyst

GitHub

A tool to analyse
the social equitability of
urban transport systems

Access to good walking and cycling paths, and to public transport, are not equally distributed throughout most cities. These unequal distributions are often related to other measures of social inequality. Urban Transport Analyst (UTA) is a tool to assess the social equity of transport infrastructure. The effects of any proposed changes to transport infrastructure can be assessed in terms of their social equitability, both as aggregated across entire cities, and in fine-scaled local detail.

Main website now at https://urbananalyst.city
